Octant

Octant enum

An arc of a circle equal to one-eigh of its circumference.

The octants are arranged as follows, with the origin (0, 0) at the center of the diagram, where the x-axis and y-axis intersect; each quadrant is further divided into two octants, resulting in a total of eight setions, and numbered counter-clockwise from the top-right corner (I):

      ^ y-axis
 \  3 | 2  /
   \  |  /
  4  \|/  1
-------------+ x-axis
  5  /|\  8
   /  |  \
 /  6 | 7  \

See also en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Circular_sector.
